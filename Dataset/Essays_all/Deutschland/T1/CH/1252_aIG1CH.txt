The Chevalier de Seingalt met very famous people like kings and writers, but some critics claimed that he distorted or invented many events to his memoire to make his life seem more exciting. He claimed in his memoir that he was very wealthy, but for real he borrowed considerable sums of money from a Swiss merchant. And when he was really rich, he would not have needed to borrow money. Also the memoir claims that the Chevalier met the writer Voltaire, but no one doubts that they had ever met. The memoir also claims that the Chevalier escaped from a notorious prison in Venice and he claims that he used an piece of metal to make a hole into the ceiling and that he climped through the roof to escape.
