An important topic nowadays is the idea of a computerized voting system which should replace the old traditional voting system. There are many people who like the idea of a computerized voting system but there are also people who are against it and prefer the old traditional system due to the following reasons.
People say that the computerized voting system is more easy to use for people who can't see very well than the traditional one. Therefore they suggest that their would be less mistakes. But critcs say that there would rather be more mistakes because of people who are not used to using computers and have no experience with technology. 
Moreover people who stand up for a computerized voting system say that such a sytsem would remove the possibilty of human error. Nevertheless critics say that also in a computerized voting sytsem errors can appear with much more heavy consequences. In addition they say that a computer can never be fully trusted.
