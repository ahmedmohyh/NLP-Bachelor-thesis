They discuss, whether a computerized counting system for votes should be used in elections or not. On the one hand critics say that the traditonal counting system is inefficient and inaccurate, so it could be very useful, because the most mistakes whilst counting are made by humans. On the other hand,as said in the audio file, there could be enormous computer errors, which could led to a completely failure of the counting system and lead to a completely wrong voting. In the text it is said that elder person could have problems by reading the sheet of paper and mark the wrong name because of their poor or less eyesight, but in the audio file it is mentioned that elder persons could have several problems while using a computerized system, because they are not used to it. They dont know how to use a computerized system and as such there could be miscounts too.
