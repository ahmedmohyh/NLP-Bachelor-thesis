Current voting systems can cause many mistakes in votings. For example it can cause a wrong vote or it might be difficult to count the given votes. With new computerized voting systems, the mistakes in votings should be less than before. Nearly everyone should understand the system. But it can also cause trouble for people who are not familiar with new technologies, for example old people who had never been in contact with computer systems or tablets before. the main aspect for computerized voting systems is the way of counting the  given votes. counting the handwritten marks on the voting ballots is difficult and takes a lot of time, while the computerized voting system count all votes in just a few seconds, essential here is that counting mistakes will not happen. So there are pros and cons in computerized voting being important in votes.
