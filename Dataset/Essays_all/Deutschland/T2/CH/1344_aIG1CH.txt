The presentation and the text were about the man Chevelier de Seingalt. He lived from 1725 to 1798 in Switzerland and he was a writer. He wrote a long memoir recounting his life an adventures.

At first Chevelier was a controversal figure, but after he met some famous people like other writers and kings, he has become a valuable historical source about European society in the eighteen century. He wasn`t a poor guy. He lived in wealth and spend a big part from his money on big partys. 
Chevelier de Seingalt was a prisoner in a prison in Venice in Italy. Other prisoners were having much friends, but de Seingalt don`t. 
He wrote down everything he could remember from the prisontime. 
After he left the Prison he wrote a book about his Life out- and inside the prison and all his other adventures.
