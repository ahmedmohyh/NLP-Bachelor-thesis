The presentation is the opposition the text. Both debate on whether the United States should change the current traditional voting system in a computerized or not. The text stands for the computerized, the presentation represents the conserative side. It takes the arguments of the text and tries to give counter arguments. As a response to the in the text given argumentation about errors, the presentation mentions, that computers can do bigger errors as people if the system is hacked or there is a bug in the software. Further it says, that old people and those who don't use the computer on a daily basis would be far more affected to make mistakes as with the current voting system.
