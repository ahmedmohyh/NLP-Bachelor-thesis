The most important skill of a teacher is that he can explain the things in an easy and effective way to the class he teaches. 
The knowledge is less important, because when he can't explain it, the knowledge is completely useless. It's because of that the ability of explaining is so crucial. At first I felt pity with them but later I was furious, because most of these teachers gave me the feeling, that they aren't trying to change their way of  teaching things.
